About Us

NM North Construction LTD is a premier construction and remodeling company dedicated to transforming residential and commercial spaces into modern, functional environments.

With over 20+ years of experience, our team has completed hundreds of projects, including kitchen renovations, bathroom upgrades, and full home remodels. We deliver tailored solutions that reflect each client’s vision.

Our services include:

  • New Home Builds: High-quality custom homes from the ground up.
  • Home Renovations: Upgrade your home’s style and comfort.
  • Home Extensions: Seamless expansions to gain extra space.
  • Loft Conversions: Turn unused lofts into functional rooms.
  • Refurbishments: Refresh old spaces with modern finishes.
  • Plumbing & Electrical: Certified, safe, and reliable services.
  • All Building Works: From small repairs to large projects.

We use high-quality materials, sustainable practices, and innovative techniques to ensure lasting value and satisfaction. At Skyline Construction, your dream home becomes our mission.
